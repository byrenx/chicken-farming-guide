### Pisik at Halak

- Amtyl 500mg tablet
- If severe use l-spec

### Fowl Cholera
- Ambroxitil
- Amtyl 500mg tablet
- If severe use l-spec

- Ambroxitil
- Amtyl 500mg tablet

### Infectious Corryza

- Amtyl 500mg tablet
- If severe use l-spec
